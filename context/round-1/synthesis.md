## Prose digest

**What's converging:** all four personas agree the typed tagged-template primitive (p1) is the foundational bet and the other two proposals are correctly framed as layers on top of it. There's strong cross-proposal agreement that the right MVP shape across the whole session is OSS-first, primitive-first, with hosted/paid layers earning their place only after adoption signal. Three of four explicitly named the TS Playground link as the launch artifact for p1.

**What's still contested:** (1) **distribution strategy for p1** — Matt and Theo treat it as a standalone primitive that earns adoption via being a 2KB dep; Guillermo argues it's a blog post unless framed as an AI SDK integration; Addy wants concrete data (a real-codebase before/after) before amplifying it. We landed on standalone package with the AI SDK before/after as the headline demo, which honors all four. (2) **launch order for p2** — Guillermo's "GitHub App first" position is a real dissent against the OSS-CLI-first majority, preserved for round 2. (3) **whether p3 should exist at all in this round** — Matt and Theo were close to "kill, ship as part of p1's scope"; Addy and Guillermo see it as a real wedge but only post-p1. The iterate verdict keeps it alive at heavily reduced scope.
